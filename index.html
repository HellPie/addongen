AddonGen A simple generator for BlockLauncher's Addons.
Addon Name
This is the name of your addon, it will be shown in BlockLauncher's addons Settings, you may want to synthesize in a word the concept of your addon, what it is all about.

You can also put your name there, to let anyone else know who made that mod, but remember not to go over 50 characters in total to not occupy too much space!
Hey! You! I said AVOID going over 50 characters, please! At least try!
Addon Package Name
This is the name that must be unique to your addon. You cannot have any other addon with this same name.

Being addons Android apps and being Android apps custom Java packets, your name must follow the Java Package Naming convention, this means that:
  • The name should be only composed by lowercase characters
  • The name should be your reversed website domain (example.com -> com.example) followed by the name of your addon (com.example.addonname, com.example.addon.name, etc)
  • If you do not have a website, you can use your email in reverse followed by the name of your addon (me[at]mail.x -> x.mail.me.addonname) or other accounts you own (github.com/me -> com.github.me.addonname, for example)
  • You can also make up your own package name, as long as it isn't a single word, it is dot-separated (so avoid spaces, slashes, other characters) and it only contains letters, numbers and underscores.

MC:PE Compatible Version
You will also need to specify the version your addon was built for, keep in mind this not just for extra fancyness! BlockLauncher will not enable your addon if the version of MC:PE installed by the user does not precisely match the one specified by your addon.

You also cannot specify multiple version of MC:PE in any possible way.

If your addon is for an alpha build of MC:PE, also remember that the MC:PE version you want to type in here is composed following this example: 0.12.1.b11!
